In an organizational network, network attacks occur when unauthorized access is made to digital assets. Passive network attacks result in malicious parties obtaining unauthorized access to networks, monitoring, and stealing private data without taking any steps to change the data. Data may be modified, encrypted, or damaged as part of a network attack.
