Sponsored by Iranian cultural office in Baghdad, a handbook entitled "Imam Khamenei in Others Quotations" published and released in Arabic language in Iraq.
According to the public relations office of the Islamic Culture and Relations Organization (ICRO), the book covers speeches and Quotations of great Islamic and International figures about the Islamic Revolutions Supreme Leader Ayatoolah Seyyed Ali Khamenei.
The "Imam Khamenei in Others Quotations" includes Quotations of great Islamic Marja including Golpayegani, Sistani, Behjat, Araki, Marashi Najafi, Javadi Amouli, Fazel Lankarani and great Ayatollahs including Meshkini, Seyyed Mahmoud Hashemi, Mesbah Yazdi and international figures like Vladimir Putin, Javier Pérez de Cuéllar and .... other worlds figures.
